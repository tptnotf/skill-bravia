## About
Control your Sony Bravia Smart TV with your voice. Turn on/off the TV, change channel, control volume, open apps and much more.

## Examples
* "Turn on the TV"
* "Mute the TV"
* "Turn the volume up on the livingroom TV"
* "Open YouTube on the TV"
